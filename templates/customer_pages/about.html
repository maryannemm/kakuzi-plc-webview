About Kakuzi PLC

Kakuzi PLC is a leading agricultural company in Kenya, specializing in the cultivation, processing, and marketing of high-quality agricultural products. With decades of experience in the industry, we are committed to sustainable farming practices, environmental conservation, and community development.

Our diversified product portfolio includes avocados, macadamia nuts, pineapples, and forestry products. We take pride in delivering premium-quality products to local and international markets while prioritizing social responsibility and ethical business practices.

At Kakuzi PLC, we strive for excellence in every aspect of our operations, from farm management and production to distribution and customer service. Our dedicated team of professionals works tirelessly to ensure the success and growth of our company while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and transparency.

We are committed to making a positive impact on the communities where we operate, promoting economic empowerment, education, and healthcare initiatives. Through our corporate social responsibility programs, we aim to create lasting value for our stakeholders and contribute to the sustainable development of Kenya.
